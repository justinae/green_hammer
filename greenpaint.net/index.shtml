Welcome to Green Paint,

Breathe deep. Breathe clean. Since 2000, Green Paint has protected you, your family and our environment by using the finest natural paints and finishes. Why add unnecessary toxins to your home? Call the experienced professionals today at (503) 317.8667.

Green Paint provides professional painting services for interiors, exteriors, cabinetry and casework for contractors and home owners. For over eight years now we have specialized in low and zero VOC content paints and finishes. We pride ourselves on our knowledge of how the variety of natural and zero VOC paint and finish products perform in real world applications. Long-term integrity and performance of our paint services is the foundation upon which our business has been built - assuring the job will be done with excellence the first time around. We provide a five-year guarantee on all work.

We are confident that we can paint any interior or exterior component of your home with nontoxic paints and match the highest level of quality and longevity of performance that you could expect from any other pain product on the market. using environmentally responsible paint does not mean the beauty or vibrancy of the finished product will in any way be compromised. Further, with the range of products available it is important to go with experienced craftsmen who have hands-on knowledge of the most recent advances in environmentally-friendly paint technology to ensure selection of the most durable and reliable paints.

We have experience with LEED projects and can ensure we meet all of your prescribed LEED requirements for your certified project.

Green Paint trains our staff on lead safe practices to ensure that we do not compromise the health and safety of our clients or our crews when working on your project. We do lead testing on all applicable projects and if the project tests positive we have a lead safe action plan for your project and will guide you through our process to ensure a safe and healthy project every time.

The health of the home, homeowner and environment are all of critical concern during the process of working with paints and finishes, which is why we only use natural and low-VOC products.

If you seek experienced, professional, high quality, ecologically conscious services we are your paint contractor!
