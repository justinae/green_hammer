New Modern Green Home in NE

A new modern home built upon an existing foundation. This house was framed using 100% locally salvaged wood from Urban Timberworks as well as wood from the previously existing house. Green materials and systems include: low density icynene spray foam insulation, rain screen ventilation under the siding, standing seam metal roof, high efficiency mini split heat pump for heating and cooling. The new home is sleek, modern and very cool. See the portfolio.
