This project was done in partnership with Jonathon Cohen and his team at Imagine Energy. SW Portland LEED Platinum Gut-Rehab

One of the nations first LEED Platinum remodels recently completed by Green Hammer in collaboration with the Paolo Design Group won the 2009 Oregon Remodelers Association Outstanding Remodeling Achievement Award for best Green Building Remodel. This outstanding project is an excellent testament to designer Paul Scardina "Paolo" ability to transform an outdated dysfunctional and inefficient space into one of our nations premier examples of high performance home conversion and how to maximize spaces while giving them a grand and elegant feel.
