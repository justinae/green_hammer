Green features

ENERGY

  • 90+% Efficient gas boiler heats both hydronic floors and panels, and domestic hot water
  • Envelope insulated with low density spray foam insulation to 40% above code (R28 walls, R40+ ceilings)
  • High performance advance framing techniques coupled with our strap wall system dramatically reduces thermal bridging
  • Reflective metal roof with air gap to reduce summer heat gain
  • Panasonic WhisperGreen™ bath fans run continuously ventilating without an HRV; motion sensors increase fan speed during use
  • High-efficiency lighting design uses Energy Star lighting for maximum performance-per-Watt
  • Energy Star appliances
  • Small home: At 1866 sq' with five bedrooms (okay one is actually the laundry room, but without the laundry equipment it is a legal bedroom;->) and two full bathrooms this home is an extremely efficient space for a large family.

WATER

  • 26 gauge standing seam Taylor metal roof allows for future potential and galvanized gutters optimized for rainwater collection (1,500 gallons per inch of rain)
  • Dual-flush toilets and low-flow plumbing fixtures

MATERIALS

  • Structure, upstairs floors, exterior decking, countertops, trim and kitchen cabinets made from local, Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) certified wood products.
  • Great care has been taken to eliminate potential for Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC's) from off gassing into the home post construction with special attention to solid wood products and low or zero VOC paints, glues, and finishes.

SITE

  • Infill project increases the density of the neighborhood.
  • Front yard to be organic edible garden.
  • Close to bus stops and has a home office, relieving dependence on a vehicle
  • Native and drought tolerant landscaping
