Green features

ENERGY

  • Ground-source heat pump heats and cools indoor air and heats domestic hot water
  • High efficiency all-electric house eliminates combustion of fossil fuels in the home and improves indoor air quality
  • 2.4 -kilowatt grid-tied PV solar system means close to net-zero annual energy consumption
  • Envelope super insulated with R25 walls, R40+ ceilings
  • High performance staggered-stud walls allow continual thermal barrier with minimal thermal bridging
  • Heat Recovery Ventilator moves stale contaminated air from the indoor environment and exhanges it with fresh oxygen rich outdoor air through a heat exchanger to minimize any energy lost in heating or cooling the incoming fresh air.
  • High-efficiency lighting design uses Energy Star, compact fluorescent, and LED lighting for maximum performance-per-Watt
  • Energy Star fiberglass windows
  • Energy Star appliances
  • Induction cooktop (high efficiency, precise adjustment, and no toxic combustion gases as with natural gas)

WATER

  • Rain Garden storm water management system to slow down, clean, and temper any site run-off.
  • Site includes a riparian zone that was completely restored with the help of DeSANTIS Landscapes .
  • Dual-flush toilets and low-flow plumbing fixtures

MATERIALS

  • All new wood material is local, Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) certified lumber (Douglas fir, hemlock) and FSC no-added-formaldehyde plywood
  • Countertops made from Cesearstone

SITE

  • Site includes a riparian zone that was completely restored with the help of DeSANTIS Landscapes .
  • Close to bus stops and has a home office, relieving dependence on a vehicle
  • Native and drought tolerant landscaping
