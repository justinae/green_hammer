Home Performance Analysis Overview

Priorities:

Comfort--Safety--Health--Energy Efficiency--Durability

HP Assessment Testing Points:

  • House Air Leakage(Blower Door Test)
  • Duct System Air Leakage(Duct Blaster Test)
  • House Insulation Levels
  • Duct System Insulation Levels
  • Mechanical System(Furnace/Heat Pump)
  • Mechanical System Safety Efficiency
  • Carbon Monoxide Testing
  • Gas Leak Detection
  • Mechanical and Passive Ventilation Levels
  • Moisture Issues

Typical Potential Upgrades

(Listed in Order of Priority)

  1. House Air Sealing
  2. Duct Sealing
  3. Attic/Roof Insulation
  4. Floor Insulation
  5. Mechanical System Upgrade
  6. Wall Insulation
  7. Water Heater Upgrade
  8. Window Replacement
  9. Ventilation System
  10. Installation Moisture Control Strategies

Typical Weatherization Project Budget

$3,000-20,000

Typical State/Federal Incentives Available

$500-3500

Typical Project Duration

1-4 weeks
